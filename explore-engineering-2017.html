Dear Educator,

This summer, the University of Connecticut is excited to again offering Explore Engineering (E2), a week-long residential program for current 10th and 11th grade students interested in engineering.

This fun program allows students to explore various engineering disciplines on the University of Connecticut main campus in Storrs. Since engineering is not a taught discipline in high school, this program exposes many students to the field for the first time. It often has a profound effect on students' future field of study and career.

There will be two sessions this year:

  • Sunday, June 18th to Thursday, June 22nd, 2017
  • Sunday, June 25th to Thursday, June 29th, 2017

The application deadline has been extended June 1, 2017.

Attached is a program flyer to provide you and your students more information.

Additional information, including testimonials and application instructions are available at: http://edoc.engr.uconn.edu/explore-engineering/.
