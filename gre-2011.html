UConn Engineering is recognized for its outstanding combination of innovative research and faculty excellence, competitive graduate support, a vibrant campus and community, and proximity to major urban centers.

  • We offer nine Ph.D. and M.S. degree programs in core and interdisciplinary subjects. Our faculty members are superbly trained, widely respected, and involved in exciting strategic research in areas such as nanotechnology, sustainable engineering, security technologies, wireless and sensor network technologies, informatics and bioinformatics, tissue engineering and regenerative medicine, to name a few.
  • Nestled amid handsome forests and farmlands, UConn also offers close proximity to the urban centers of Hartford, Boston and New York City. The UConn community also provides exciting cultural and athletic activities, dedicated housing for graduate student families as well as individual students, and excellent K-12 schools that are ranked among the top in the State. Take a virtual tour of campus here.
  • Ph.D. students receive competitive stipends and excellent health insurance. Many students also receive prestigious graduate fellowships. In the past two years, we have captured five Graduate Assistance in Areas of National Need (GAANN) awards in the areas of biomaterials, sustainable energy, advanced computing, security and cloud computing. In 2010, we garnered an NSF GK-12 award (total $2.7M), which partners graduate students in engineering with technical high school teachers and students. Through these programs, more than 45 fellows are supported per year.
